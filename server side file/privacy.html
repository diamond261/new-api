Privacy & Data

Last updated: 2026-04-10 (HKT)  |  最後更新：2026-04-10（香港時間）
This privacy policy explains how Model Bay collects, uses, stores, and protects information in connection with our API platform services. It is drafted in the general compliance spirit of the Hong Kong Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486).

1. Data We Collect / 收集資料範圍

  • Account information — Login identifiers and contact details (if provided).
    帳戶資料：登入識別資料、聯絡方式（如提供）。
  • Service operation data — API request timestamps, model names, error codes, quota and usage statistics.
    服務營運資料：API 請求時間、模型名稱、錯誤碼、配額及用量統計。
  • Security & risk control data — IP addresses, device and session identifiers for abuse prevention, anomaly detection, and security auditing.
    安全與風險控制資料：IP、裝置與會話識別資訊。

2. Purpose of Use / 使用目的

We process data only to the extent reasonably necessary for: providing and maintaining the API service, authentication and authorization, request routing, troubleshooting, billing, risk control, and fulfilling legal and regulatory obligations.

我們僅於合理及必要範圍內處理資料，用於提供服務、認證與授權、請求路由、故障排查、帳務與風險控制、履行法律及監管責任。

3. Retention & Deletion / 保留期限與刪除

Data is retained only for as long as needed to fulfill the processing purpose. When no longer required, we delete, anonymize, or de-identify it in accordance with our internal procedures.

資料僅於達成處理目的所需期間內保存。當不再需要時，我們會進行刪除、匿名化或去識別化處理。

4. Third-Party Processing / 第三方處理與披露

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Disclosure occurs only where required by law, with your explicit consent, or where necessary to provide the service (e.g., cloud infrastructure providers bound by confidentiality and security obligations).

除法律規定、你明示同意或屬提供服務所必要外，我們不會向第三方出售你的個人資料。

5. Cross-Border Transfers / 跨境傳輸

Cloud infrastructure may involve cross-border processing. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures and, where practicable, ensure that recipients adopt appropriate safeguards.

雲端基礎設施可能涉及跨境處理，我們會採取合理技術及組織措施確保適當保障。

6. Your Rights / 你的權利

Where permitted by applicable law, you may request access to and correction of your personal data. We will process such requests within a reasonable timeframe.

在適用法律容許下，你可就你的個人資料提出查閱及更正要求。

7. Contact & Updates / 聯絡與更新

We may update this policy as required by law or service needs. The updated version will be published on this page. See also: Terms of Service / 使用條款.
